Star Wars: Episode IV - A New Hope

Released May 25, 1977

Action /Adventure / Fantasy / Sci-Fi |

A young farmer named Luke joins a mission to rescue Princess Leia from the clutches of the evil Darth Vader. With the help of a wise old mentor named Obi-Wan Kenobi, a smuggler named Han Solo, and a droid duo, R2-D2 and C-3PO, they set out on an adventure across galaxies to deliver secret plans that could save the galaxy. They face thrilling battles and meet strange creatures, all while striving to defeat the oppressive Empire and its planet-destroying weapon. As they work together, Luke discovers his own hidden potential and learns about the power of the Force. The movie is a tale of heroes, villains, and the struggle between good and evil in a faraway galaxy.
